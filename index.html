What is PixelCafe?

PixelCafe is a unique productivity app that combines focus timers, habit tracking, journaling, and flashcards with an adorable pixel cafe simulation. As you complete tasks and build healthy habits, your virtual cafe grows and attracts more customers!

How to Use PixelCafe

  • Focus Sessions: Set a timer (15-60 minutes) and focus on your work. You'll earn coins every 40 minutes!
  • Habits: Create daily habits with optional time windows (e.g., "Morning Exercise" only available 6-10 AM)
  • Cafe Growth: Your productivity directly affects your cafe's popularity and attracts more customers
  • Shopping: Use earned coins to buy tables, decorations, and hire more staff
  • Level Up: Each hour of total focus time advances your cafe level (max level 20)

Coins & Progression

  • Earn 60 coins for every 40 minutes of focused work
  • Get 100 bonus coins when you level up
  • Completing habits boosts your cafe's popularity
  • Higher popularity attracts more customers to your tables

Troubleshooting

  • Timer not working: Make sure notifications are enabled for the app
  • Habits locked: Check if the habit has time restrictions and is currently available
  • Characters not moving: This is normal - characters move at different intervals for realism
  • Data not saving: The app automatically saves your progress locally on your device

What Data We DON'T Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Your tasks, habits, or journal entries
  • Any data from imported PDFs or notes
  • Device information beyond what's necessary for app function

How Your Data is Stored

  • All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and local storage
  • Your cafe progress, habits, tasks, and journal entries never leave your device
  • When you delete the app, all data is permanently removed
  • No cloud backups or external servers are used

Permissions We Use

  • Notifications: To remind you about focus sessions and send motivational messages
  • File Access: Only when you choose to import PDFs for reading/studying
Important: Since all data is stored locally, make sure to backup your device if you want to preserve your cafe progress when switching phones!
